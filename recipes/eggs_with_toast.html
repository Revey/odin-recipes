Breakfast Eggs

Descrption

I eat this meal almost every morning. This meal consist of a little over 300 calories. I focus mainly on getting a good amount of protein to start off my day. Since this meal focuses mainly on a low calorie but high protein route it contains barely any fat and mainly protien and carbs.

Ingredients

  • Liquid Egg Whites (230 grams)
  • One whole egg
  • Toast (2 slices)

Steps

  1. Mix the egg with the egg white
  2. Heat up a pan on medium and spray with pan oil spray (using oil spray keeps the amount of calories from oil to a minumum)
  3. Put the bread slices in a toaster
